Meet Dr. J

Akinloye Julius Makanjuola, M.D., F.C.C.P.

Dr. Akinloye J. Makanjuola, or Dr. J, is a board certified Pulmonary & Critical Care physician. His deep knowledge of medicine, outstanding bedside manners and infectious personality has earned him recognition and respect among his patients and colleagues alike. Dr. Makanjuola received his medical degree at the University College Hospital, University of Ibadan, Nigeria. He completed an Internal Medicine residency at Texas Tech University Health Science Center, where he served as Chief Resident and received numerous awards and accolades including Outstanding Intern of the Year, Resident of the Year, and the Resident’s Choice Award. Following residency, Dr. Makanjuola spent a year as an Internist at the Navajo Indian Reservation‘s Northern Navajo Medical Center in Shiprock, New Mexico. Thereafter, he successfully completed a three-year Pulmonary and Critical Care fellowship at Louisiana State University Health Science Center where he also served as Chief Fellow. Dr. Makanjuola now operates his private practice, Georgia Universal Lungs, LLC, at the Emory Midtown Hospital Medical Office Towers in Midtown Atlanta.
Dr. Makanjuola, also board certified in Internal Medicine, has been published in peer review medical journals and has presented at medical conferences. He is an attending physician at Atlanta Medical Center where he engages in the training of medical residents. Additionally, he is a Medical Consultant for Georgia Disability Adjudication Services, serves as the Medical Director of Briarcliff Haven Nursing Home Ventilator Unit as well as the Director of the Respiratory Therapist training program at the Everest College in Decatur. Dr. Makanjuola is a member of American College of Chest Physicians, American College of Physicians, Association of Nigerian Physicians in the Americas and the American Medical Association.
Dr. Makanjuola is a devoted husband and father of two young sons.
